RISKS ASSOCIATED WITH SYSTEMIC MEDICATIONS

Oral NSAIDs

  • Bleeding, Gl dysfunction, CV events, renal injury
  • Risks in people over age 60: personal/family history of peptic ulcers or Gl bleeding, cardiovascular and cerebrovascular disease, diabetes mellitus, antiplatelet drugs, Helicobacter pylori infection, hypocholesterolemia, and NSAIDs (up to 3 months) 8

Acetaminophen

Pervasive use in OTC and Rx products
  • 2nd most common cause of liver transplantation worldwide and the most common in the US.
  • Responsible for 56,000 ED visits, 2600 hospitalizations, and 500 deaths per year in the US.
  • Fifty percent of overdoses are unintentional 9 (narrow therapeutic window)
